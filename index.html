[ FAQ ]

Quick answers.

What kind of brands do you work with?

Founder-led D2C, healthcare and B2B brands doing roughly €30k–€500k/month, ready to invest in scaling.

How does pricing work?

We'd rather walk you through it on the call — pricing depends on scope, channels and what you actually need. No enterprise pricing games, just a straight number once we understand the work.

Paid media only, or full funnel?

Single channel works. The full system — brand, creative, media, web, analytics — is where compounding happens.

What if we're starting from zero?

If you're ready to invest, we'll build the whole machine from scratch — brand, site, Meta, Google, TikTok, analytics. Day one to first sale to scale, handled by the two of us end-to-end.

How fast can we launch?

Audit week one. Rebuild week two–three. Live by week four.

Who actually does the work?

The two founders. Nikola — web, brand, CRO. Daniel — media, creative, analytics. No juniors. No offshore.

What if results don't show up?

30-day pilot exits cleanly with month-one fees refunded if we don't move the metric we agreed on.

Do you sign NDAs?

Always. Mutual NDA before any account access.
